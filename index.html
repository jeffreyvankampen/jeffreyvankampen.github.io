Introduction. To give an idea about what I do. So, what happened before?

My name is Jeffrey. I am a student at the Hogeschool van Amsterdam where I direct myself to interaction, projects and programming of interactive design. In 2015 I started my first study and got to work with Adobe Photoshop. And after one hour of doing random stuff, I absolutely fell in love with it. Sadly, they didn’t teach me a lot about Photoshop. That’s why I started learning myself, just by using it a much! In the first year I didn’t improve much. However, around the next year things started getting to look more and more decent.

Studying now at the Hogeschool van Amsterdam things became more than visual design. I started to think about centering on the user. Like the experience and interaction with any platform. About ideas and how to structure interactive design. That may sound very unlike any study but it is what I specialise in.
